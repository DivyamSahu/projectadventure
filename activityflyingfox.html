Longest Flying Fox (1250 ft)

Are you a Thrill Seeker? It's Time To Experience India's Longest Flying Fox, an exhilarating 1250 Ft of adrenaline! Soar above the park on a series of ultra-strong steel cables, attached by a harness and pulley, taking in the bird's eye view of the scenery around you, zipping along the Flying Fox adventure. State-of-the-art harnesses and braking systems make this a hands-free, hair-raising ride you have to experience to believe. During the activity, you're supervised 100% of the time by our trained marshals.
